# 📊 Flipkart Product & Review Analysis

## 🔍 Project Overview

This project analyzes Flipkart product data to uncover insights around pricing, customer ratings, and brand performance. The dashboard helps identify how product price impacts customer perception and which brands dominate in terms of volume and ratings.

The goal of this project is to transform raw product-level data into meaningful business insights using data visualization.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Understand the relationship between **price bands and customer ratings**
* Analyze **review volume distribution across price segments**
* Identify **top-performing brands based on product volume**
* Compare **brand-wise average ratings**
* Provide a **quick KPI snapshot** for decision-making

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 KPI Summary

* Total Reviews
* Average Price
* Average Rating
* Total Products

### 🔹 Rating by Price Band

* Shows how customer ratings vary across different price ranges
* Helps identify whether expensive products are rated higher

### 🔹 Review Volume by Price Band

* Displays the number of reviews across price categories
* Highlights which price segments drive the most engagement

### 🔹 Top Brands (by Volume)

* Identifies brands with the highest number of products/reviews
* Helps understand market dominance

### 🔹 Brand Rating

* Compares average ratings across different brands
* Highlights best-performing brands in terms of customer satisfaction

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Lower price bands generate the highest number of reviews, indicating higher purchase volume
* Higher price bands generally have better average ratings
* A few brands dominate product volume, suggesting strong market presence
* Brand performance varies significantly in terms of customer ratings
